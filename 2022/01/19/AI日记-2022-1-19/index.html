AI日记

English learning

supervise

监督；管理；指导；主管

to be in charge of sb/sth and make sure that everything is done correctly, safely, etc.

map sth on/onto sth

把…与…相联系

to link a group of qualities, items, etc. with their source, cause, position on a scale, etc.

discrete

(formal or technical 术语) 分离的；互不相连的；各别的

independent of other things of the same type

humidity

（空气中的）湿度

the amount of water in the air

approximate

近似；接近

to be similar or close to sth in nature, quality, amount, etc., but not exactly the same

[VN] 近似计算；概略估算

to calculate or estimate sth fairly accurately

plot

（在地图上）画出，标出

~ sth (on sth)

to mark sth on a map, for example the position or course of sth

The earthquake centres had been plotted on a world map.

地震震中均被标示在一张世界地图上。

绘制（图表）

to make a diagram or chart from some information

We carefully plotted each patient’s response to the drug on a chart.

我们仔细绘出了每个病人对这种药物反应的图表。

绘制（曲线）

to mark points on a graph and draw a line or curve connecting them

First, plot the temperature curve on the graph.

首先，在图表上绘出温度曲线来。

strategy

策略；计策

[C] ~ (for doing sth) | ~ (to do sth)

intuition

（一种）直觉

[C] ~ (that…)

an idea or a strong feeling that sth is true although you cannot explain why

I had an intuition that something awful was about to happen.

我直觉感到要出乱子了。

get around

克服，解决（问题或困难）

To get around a problem or difficulty means to overcome it.

None of these countries has found a way yet to get around the problem of the polarization of wealth.

这些国家都还没有找到办法来解决贫富分化的问题。

规避，避开（法律或规章）

If you get around a rule or law, you find a way of doing something that the rule or law is intended to prevent, without actually breaking it.

Although tobacco ads are prohibited, companies get around the ban by sponsoring music shows.

虽然严禁为烟草做广告，但一些烟草公司却通过赞助音乐会来打擦边球。

（消息等）传开，散播出去

If news gets around, it becomes well known as a result of being told to lots of people.

They threw him out because word got around that he was taking drugs…

由于有传言说他吸毒，他们把他解雇了。

hypotheses

[C] （有少量事实依据但未被证实的）假说，假设

an idea or explanation of sth that is based on a few known facts but that has not yet been proved to be true or correct

yield

[VN] 出产（作物）；产生（收益、效益等）；提供

to produce or provide sth, for example a profit, result or crop

Higher-rate deposit accounts yield good returns.

高利率的存款会产生丰厚的收益。

The research has yielded useful information.

这项研究提供了有用的资料。

trees that no longer yield fruit

不再结果实的树

屈服；让步

[V] ~ (to sth/sb)

to stop resisting sth/sb; to agree to do sth that you do not want to do

takeaway

心得；领悟；总结

an important fact, point or idea to be remembered from talk, meeting or event

leeway

自由活动的空间

the amount of freedom that you have to change sth or to do sth in the way you want to

margin

[usually sing.] 余地；备用的时间（或空间、金钱等）

an extra amount of sth such as time, space, money, etc. that you include in order to make sure that sth is successful

a safety margin

安全距离

(formal) 边缘；极限；界限

the extreme edge or limit of a place

the eastern margin of the Indian Ocean

印度洋的东岸

repression

倒退；回归；退化

[U, C] ~ (to sth)
the process of going back to an earlier or less advanced form or state

overfit

过拟合

Supervised Learning(监督学习)

简单来说就是输入一个数据集，数据集中每个数据都是函数的(自变量，函数值)对，然后通过这个数据集来给出新的输入的输出。(这里假设函数值是二值的)

Nearest-Neighbor Classification(最邻近分类)

对于新的输入，在数据集中找到与其最相邻的点，将这个点的函数值作为输出。

很显然这个方法是很狭隘的。

改进策略：k-邻近分类

Perceptron Learning(感知机算法)

感知机算法的前提是类别线性可分，也就是能用一条直线(一个平面等等)把数据集中不同输出的点分成两块
拟合出来的直线有如下形式：

w₀ + w₁x₁ + w₂x₂

每遇到一个新数据，更新权重wi

wi = wi + α·sign(y - h(x)) × xi

其中，sign()是符号函数，h(x)是这个新数据的预测函数值,α是学习系数，需要调参23333~

Support Vector Machines(支持向量机)

能够找出最好的分割边界(Maximum Margin Separator)，还可以作出非线性边界。

Loss Functions(损失函数)

损失函数用来衡量预测值与实际值的差值，可以用差绝对值、差的平方，用平方会更加严厉的惩罚异常值
